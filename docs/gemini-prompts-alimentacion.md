# Prompts Gemini - Alimentación

Estos son los prompts de referencia usados para generar las imágenes de la sección de producto en `sectores index/alimentacion.html`.

## 01. Harinas, mezclas y preparados

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: website card image for a B2B private label food manufacturing page, horizontal 16:9 composition
Primary request: Create a premium commercial product composition for a private label food manufacturing company. Show a clean studio setup with neutral light background and a cohesive family of packaging formats related to Harinas, mezclas y preparados. Include multiple realistic packaging types that fit the category, such as doypacks and bags of flour, pancake mix, dry food powder blends, a small tub, sachets, bowls with fine flour and powder, and a clean food preparation context. Make the composition elegant, minimal, high-end, and suitable for a professional industrial website. The image should look like a premium product family showcase, not a single isolated container.
Scene/backdrop: warm neutral off-white studio surface, soft shadows, clean food-grade environment, no text, no logos, no readable brand names.
Style/medium: premium private-label food catalog photography, realistic packaging, refined white/navy/gold accents.
Composition/framing: horizontal 16:9 card crop, all products fully visible with generous margins, no people, no watermark.
```
