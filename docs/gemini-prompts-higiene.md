Prompts de referencia para generar las imágenes de la sección de producto en `sectores index/higiene.html`.  
Carpeta de destino sugerida: `img/sectores/higiene/` (crear si no existe).

## 01. Samples y muestras de limpieza

```text
Use case: product-mockup
Asset type: website card image for a B2B private label cleaning product manufacturing page, horizontal 16:9 composition
Primary request: Create a premium commercial product composition for a private label cleaning product manufacturing company specializing in samples and trial formats. Show a clean studio setup with a cohesive family of small-format cleaning product samples: flat sachets, heat-sealed single-dose packets, miniature bottles (5–15ml), troquelado die-cut single-use packs, and small vials containing transparent and slightly tinted cleaning liquid. The arrangement should feel purposeful and premium — like what a brand manager would receive as a sampling kit for a new product launch or retail trial campaign. Multiple formats arranged elegantly, clean surfaces, some product droplets suggesting cleaning freshness.
Scene/backdrop: clean white/light neutral studio surface, soft directional light creating gentle shadows, small water droplets suggesting cleanliness, no text, no logos, no readable brand names on packaging.
Style/medium: premium B2B packaging catalog photography, realistic high-quality flexible and rigid packaging, professional industrial quality, crisp edges and materials.
Composition/framing: horizontal 16:9 card crop, all sample formats fully visible with generous margins, no people, no watermark.
```

## Notas de implementación

- Las imágenes generadas deben sustituir las imágenes de placeholder actuales en `sectores index/higiene.html`
- Integrar como `prod-card-img--photo` (cover) con `background:#0A0B1C`
- Mantener `loading="lazy"` en todos los `<img>`
- Resolución mínima recomendada: 1200×675 px (16:9) a 2× para retina
