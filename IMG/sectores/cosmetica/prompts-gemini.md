Primary request: Create a premium commercial product composition for a private label cosmetics manufacturing company. Include multiple realistic packaging types that fit the category, such as cosmetic tubes, cream jars, airless pump bottles, lotion bottles, and small premium tubs. Make the composition elegant, minimal, high-end, and suitable for a professional industrial website. The image should look like a premium product family showcase, not a single isolated container.

Style/medium: premium private-label cosmetics catalog photography, realistic packaging, refined navy/white/gold accents.
Composition/framing: horizontal 16:9 card crop, all products fully visible with generous margins, clean grouping, no people, no watermark.
```
